When you create a new wallet, a new QLC Chain Wallet seed will be generated which can be used to create brand new accounts on the QLC Chain network. Your QLC Wallet seed is the master key to all of your accounts and any money inside of them!

Make sure you back up your seed, write it down, and keep it incredibly safe! There is NO way to recover it after creation, and all funds inside WILL be lost without it!
