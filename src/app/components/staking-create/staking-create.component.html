ABOUT STAKING TYPES

FOR VOTING/MINING NODE

Minimum Amount: 1 QLC
Minimum Staking Period: 10 days
Voting Earning per day:
Your staked amount/whole network staked amount x 8,302 QGAS
Mining Earning per day:
Miners to share the pool of 8219 QGAS
Miner Earning = Voting + Mining Earning

*Stake 1 million QLC or above is entitled to be a mining node, or run a mining pool

FOR CONFIDANT STATION

Minimum Amount: 2,000 QLC
Minimum Staking Period: 90 days
Rewards: Same as voting node - QGAS consumption

*First three month promotional period QGAS consumption will be 0

Purchase a Confidant with 20% discount with Crypto Payment

FOR TOKEN MINTAGE

Minimum Amount: 500,000 QLC
Minimum Staking Period: 180 days
Rewards: Same as voting node and entitled to issue a token
